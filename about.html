Our Mission

We believe in fostering a dynamic and engaging learning environment that goes beyond the classroom. "Trivial Trivia" is designed to test and expand students' knowledge of our rich campus history, distinguished faculty, and the vibrant community that makes UofM-Flint unique. Through this interactive platform, our goal is to inspire curiosity, deepen connections to the university, and promote a sense of pride among students.By aligning with UofM's commitment to excellence in teaching and learning, we aim to: Engage students in meaningful partnerships with faculty, enhancing both academic growth and collaboration. Develop mastery and confidence in various disciplines, preparing students for a future of meaningful and productive work. Support research, creativity, and innovation through fun and challenging questions that stimulate critical thinking. Encourage students to connect their knowledge with real-world applications, promoting service and engagement both within the university and in broader society. Uphold the values of academic freedom, diversity, equality, and justice, creating an inclusive space for all participants to celebrate the UofM legacy. Through "Trivial Trivia," we invite the UofM-Flint community to celebrate its past, engage in the present, and prepare for a future shaped by knowledge, collaboration, and creativity.
