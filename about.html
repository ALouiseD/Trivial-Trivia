Our Mission

We believe in fostering a dynamic and engaging learning environment that goes beyond the classroom. "Trivial Trivia" is designed to test and expand students' knowledge of our rich campus history, and the vibrant community that makes UofM-Flint unique. Through this interactive platform, our goal is to inspire curiosity, deepen connections to the university, and promote a sense of pride among students.
